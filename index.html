About Us

Rajpoot Food Cottage Raiwind Road

Rajpoot Food Cottage (RFC) is a premier fast-food destination located on Raiwind Road, Lahore. We pride ourselves on serving fresh chicken, 100% real cheddar and mozzarella cheese, and rich signature spices. From double egg burgers to cheese-loaded stuffed crust pizzas, quality is our top priority.
